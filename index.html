About Anthony

I am a freelance individual, focused on immersive and narrative experiences using creative technologies. I work with a great team of designers, developers, artists, and technicians, and I love to collaborate with other creative individuals.

I am driven by my passion, and I am highly regarded by my clients and peers.

Let's make something together.

Azure Playkit

Azure Playkit is a piece that enables participation. The artwork is created by interacting with 3D space to paint. This allows the canvas to be stamped with colours, shapes and real-world objects, and enables group participation and play.
Out of Sight

Out of Sight is an interactive, adaptive, audio/visual VR experience. Developed alongside a musician and composer, Out of Sight is a narrative where the player investigates a haunted property. The audio adapts to the users actions, making each playthrough unique.
Submerse - Azure Playkit

The Azure Playkit Exhibition was displayed on an online 3D digital exhibition named Submerse. Azure Playkit had it's own room, much like the other works in the exhibition. The space was used to exhibit the work in the way it would have been in reality, if it wasn't for the Covid-19 pandemic.

Submerse - Brochure

Void

A VR experience designed to allow full user exploration and discovery. The experience has puzzles and secrets, with a focus of creating engaging ways of navigating virtual environments using portals.
The Weather Project Experiment

A VR experiment exploring how Virtual Reality can be used to create artwork inspired by real-world installation and participatory artwork.
